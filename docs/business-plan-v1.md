## Chosen Product: **ProposalForge**
> AI-powered proposal & contract generator for freelancers and small agencies

### Problem
Freelancers and small agencies spend 2–4 hours per proposal writing, formatting, and sending.
Current solutions: Google Docs templates (clunky), PandaDoc/DocuSign (expensive, complex), Word (manual).
**Freelancers hate proposal writing but it directly drives their income.**

### Target Customer
- Solo freelancers (designers, devs, consultants, writers)
- Small agencies (2–10 people)
- Primarily US/English-speaking market
- Willingness to pay: $20–$80/month

### Core Features
1. **Proposal templates** — pre-built + customizable
2. **AI writing assist** — generate proposal content from a brief
3. **e-Sign integration** — DocuSign / HelloSign embedded
4. **Client portal** — clients view, comment, sign online
5. **Analytics** — open rates, time spent, conversion tracking
6. **Recurring proposals** — retainer agreements

### Revenue Model
- **Freemium**: 3 proposals/month free, $29/month Pro (unlimited)
- **Add-ons**: extra templates, white-label, API access
